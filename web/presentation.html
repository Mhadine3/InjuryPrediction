Injury-risk model

How the injury model thinks

Workload is the strongest signal

Feature importance is dominated by the acute:chronic workload ratio (ACWR) — the same spike-in-load signal sports scientists watch — followed by raw load, training phase, fatigue and HRV. Every prediction is explainable, grounded in published methods rather than a black box.

ACWR · Gabbett 2016 sRPE load · Foster 2001 HRV recovery · Buchheit 2014 Hooper wellness 1995 GPS load · Bradley 2009
