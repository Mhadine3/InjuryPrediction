What it does

One platform, from training ground to the final

🩺

Injury-risk prediction

Per-player risk scores from acute:chronic workload (ACWR), wellness and GPS load — surfaced as low / moderate / high / very-high before injuries occur.

🏆

Live knockout projection

Real results stream in from the tournament; the model fills the rest of the bracket with predicted opponents and scorelines, round of 32 through the final.

🛣️

Road to the Final

Pick any team and trace its projected path — opponent and win probability at each stage — stopping the moment it's eliminated (or crowned champion).

📊

Pre-match predictions

A Dixon-Coles Poisson model gives win / draw / loss probabilities, the most-likely scoreline and player scorer odds for any fixture.

⚡

Readiness & wellness

Daily readiness from sleep, fatigue, soreness, HRV and hydration — Hooper-index wellness combined with recovery markers to flag under-recovered players.

🗺️

Match heatmaps & live tracking

Positional heatmaps and live match state, so medical and tactical staff see risk and performance in one place.

Under the hood

Backed by sports science, not guesswork

Every score is transparent and grounded in published research — the same load-monitoring methods used by elite performance departments, wired into a live ML pipeline.

XGBoost injury model Dixon-Coles Poisson ACWR · Gabbett 2016 sRPE · Foster 2001 HRV · Buchheit 2014 Hooper wellness 1995

Ingest daily load

Wellness, sRPE training load, GPS distance & sprints, HRV and hydration — one row per player per day.

Compute ACWR & features

Acute:chronic workload ratios and 40+ engineered features feed a gradient-boosted injury model (R² ≈ 0.96).

3

Predict & project

Risk scores per player, plus a Dixon-Coles engine that forecasts every match and projects the bracket to the final.
